Dr. Jesus S. Armacanqui

Dr. Armacanqui has more than 25 years of corporate experience, working globally with E & P companies such as Shell, Conoco, PDVSA and Dea Oil & Gas and major service companies such as Schlumberger, Camco-Reda. He participated in the Development of Shale Oil leases in the USA as well as in the operation of multiple tied oil fields in the Americas and around the world. He has been the senior ESP application engineer of a global ESP supplier and has worked as the ESP team leader of a global operator overseeing a team in charge of 900 ESPs. Dr. Armacanqui has also pioneered the development and operation of an Uncoventional Reservoir of Extra Heavy Oil Project in the Orinoco Belt field San Diego, Venezuela (120,000 bopd) where he was the production specialist in charge. Other assignments include technical manager of Reda Pumps, Venezuela, head of artificial lift for PDO-Oman, development manager for DEA Egypt and Coo of WOGI, USA. He has a Ph.D. in Reservoir Engineering from Freiberg Mining Academy and a Post-Doctorate in Projects & Business Management from the Clausthal University and is an international lecturer, in topics of production optimization, ESPs, PCP, BPs GL, sustainable oil field development, effective introduction of innovations/new technology, well testing, operational excellence, EOR, process enhancement, advanced tendering techniques, and oil field management in the scenario of low oil prices for heavy and shale oil fields. He has initiated/participated in the development & pilot field test of multiple innovations – world’s first prototypes. He is a regular author of SPE papers and gives advanced training on ESp and GL Advanced Level for senior staff, managers and executives for the University of Oklahoma. He has been Member of Technical Committees of SPE and AAPG Conferences, lately for AAPG GTW in China
